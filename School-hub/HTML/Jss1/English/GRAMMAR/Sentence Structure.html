3. Sentence Structure

A sentence is a group of words that express a complete thought. Sentences can be classified based on their structure:

  • Simple Sentence
  • Compound Sentence
  • Complex Sentence
  • Compound-Complex Sentence

  • Simple Sentence:A sentence with a subject and a predicate.

    • Example: She reads books.

  • Compound Sentence:A sentence with two or more independent clauses connected by a conjunction.

    • Example: I wanted to go to the beach, but it started raining.

  • Complex Sentence:A sentence with one independent clause and at least one dependent clause.

    • Example: Although it was raining, we went outside.

  • Compound-Complex Sentence: A sentence with two independent clauses and at least one dependent clause.

    • Example: I wanted to go to the beach, but it started raining, so we stayed indoors.
