



Tenses:

Choose the correct form of the verb:
"By the time I get there, they ____ (leave)."

Answer: will have left (Future Perfect)

Sentence Structure:

Combine the two sentences into one compound sentence:
"I like soccer. I don't like basketball."

Answer: I like soccer, but I don't like basketball.

Punctuation:

Correct the punctuation in the following sentence:
"Where is my book i cant find it"

Answer: "Where is my book? I can't find it."
